Shade-in Sketch

Hover mouse over grid to sketch. Press mousewheel to pause shading and left button to continue shading. With this option, each hover/stroke over the sketchpad will increase the shade of the pixels.

Trail Sketch

Hover mouse over grid to sketch. Press mousewheel to pause shading and left button to continue shading. With this option, a fading trail will follow your pointer over the grid.
